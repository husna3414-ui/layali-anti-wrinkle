How does it work?

A purified protein called Botulinum Toxin Type A is placed into selected muscles using a very fine needle, temporarily reducing muscle movement. As the muscle relaxes:

  • Wrinkles soften
  • Lines become less noticeable
  • Skin appears smoother
  • Future lines are slowed

The treatment does not fill wrinkles — instead, it treats the muscle responsible for creating them.

What happens during treatment?

Consultation

We assess your facial anatomy, muscle movement and treatment goals.

Treatment

A series of quick injections are placed into the selected muscles — most appointments take around 10–20 minutes.

Recovery

Little to no downtime. Most clients return straight to work or normal daily activities.

Frequently asked questions

Does it hurt?

Most clients describe a quick pinching sensation lasting only a few seconds.

When will I see results?

Results begin developing after 2–5 days and are fully visible at around 14 days.

How long does it last?

Typically 3–4 months.

Will people know I've had it done?

Most people simply notice you look fresher and more rested — not that you've had treatment.

Can it help teeth grinding?

Yes — masseter treatment can reduce jaw tension and teeth grinding while also slimming the jawline.

Is there any downtime?

Most clients return to normal activities immediately.
